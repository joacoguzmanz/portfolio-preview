I am a front-end developer who loves design and new technologies.

Based in Buenos Aires, I am a really curious person who is always learning and trying new things.

I am really fond in subjects like Artificial Intelligence, Augmented Reality and the Internet of Things and I would love to work in projects that implement this technologies in websites to take user experience to a new level.

Technologies I use

When I think of a project, I usually start on paper making some mockups or taking notes about the ideas I got. Then I use Whimsical to work on the sitemaps and basic structure of the site. I follow with wireframes and designs in Adobe XD and use Photoshop if there is an image that needs a little retouching.

For development, I like to use SASS to keep my CSS as clean as I can and try to make my HTML the most Google friendly possible. I also use vanilla JavaScript whenever necessary. For the back-end, I started working with Django because I love Python and I used PostgreSQL as it is Django's recommended.

Even though I do not master all of this technologies I am capable of making some good projects all by myself.
